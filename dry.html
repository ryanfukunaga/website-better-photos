Keeping the Sidewalks Dry!

  • During high school, I wanted to make my senior capstone project on creating a device that can help prevent sidewalks from flooding. I didn't know much about circuits at the time, so the electronics were not very advanced. In the future, I hope to incorporate Arduino into this project to make it more automatic. Overall, this was a good experience for me to learn about the engineering design process and learn about basic mechanical devices.
